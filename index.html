Trip to Manali

Manali, nestled in the Himalayas, is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure seekers alike.

Highlights

  • Visit the Solang Valley for skiing and paragliding.
  • Explore the serene Hidimba Devi Temple.
  • Try river rafting in the Beas River.
  • Sample delicious Himachali cuisine.
